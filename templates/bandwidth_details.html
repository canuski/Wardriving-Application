Bandwidth Information

Explanation of Bandwidth Types

  • HT160: High Throughput 160 MHz is only available in Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax) or newer.
  • HT80: High Throughput 80 MHz is supported by Wi-Fi 5 (802.11ac) and Wi-Fi 6.
  • HT40: High Throughput 40 MHz can be used by Wi-Fi 4 (802.11n) and Wi-Fi 5.
  • HT20: High Throughput 20 MHz can be used by Wi-Fi 4 (802.11n) and Wi-Fi 5.
  • Note: It is difficult to determine the exact Wi-Fi standard for HT20 and HT40 without additional information.
